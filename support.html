Before you email (bug reports)

Please include:

  • Your device model (e.g. iPhone 15)
  • iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • Runnr version (in the app, if shown in Profile / About — or say when you downloaded from the App Store)
  • What you were doing and what went wrong (screenshots help)

Common topics

  • Location: Runnr needs location access while you use run tracking or the map. You can change this anytime in the iOS Settings app under Runnr → Location.
  • Account: Sign-in options and account deletion are handled in the app (see Profile / account settings).
